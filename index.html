Privacy Policy - Discovery Mode

Last updated: September 15, 2024

What We Collect

Discovery Mode only stores the songs you've listened to on Spotify. We don't collect any personal information.

How We Use It

We use this information to skip those songs when they come back up.

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device. We don't send or store any information on external servers.

Third-Party Services

We don't use any third-party services. Discovery Mode only interacts with Spotify's web player.

Your Control

You can clear all stored data at any time by uninstalling the extension.
